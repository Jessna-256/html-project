Range Rover Autobiography

The Range Rover Autobiography is a high-end luxury SUV from Land Rover, known for its premium features, powerful performance, and advanced technology. It sits near the top of the Range Rover lineup, just below the Range Rover SV, offering a blend of luxury, off-road capability, and cutting-edge technology.

Key Features of Range Rover Autobiography

  1. Engine Options and performance
    • Mild-Hybrid & Plug-in Hybrid Options:
      • P530 – 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (530 hp, 750 Nm torque)
      • P550e – 3.0L Inline-6 Plug-in Hybrid (550 hp, 800 Nm torque)
    • Diesel Options (Select Markets):
      • D350 – 3.0L Inline-6 Diesel (350 hp, 700 Nm torque)
    • All-Wheel Drive (AWD) and 8-speed automatic transmission with adaptive dynamics.
